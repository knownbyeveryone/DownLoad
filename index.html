About Us

GeoSensing and Imaging Consultancy provides satellite Earth observation services, performs research and development, and advises stakeholders in the interpretation and integration of Earth observation products for land, water and air.

We offer the best consultancy services in Image Processing, Geo-information Systems and Remote sensing applications through Artificial intelligence, Machine learning and Deep learning.We have highly skilled and experienced experts in Computer science, Digital signal processing and Remote sensing.

Our services, among others include: field data collection, facility/resource mapping, Web mapping, Spatial database development, satellite image processing.
